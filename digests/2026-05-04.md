# AI Daily Digest — May 4, 2026

**TL;DR:** OpenAI rolled out GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.5 Pro to paid ChatGPT tiers, retiring older models and expanding context windows up to 400k tokens. · AI music startup Suno is closing a funding round at a valuation north of $5 billion, while defense-tech darling Anduril nears a $60 billion raise led by a16z and Thrive. · xAI sued to block Colorado's AI Act, the DOJ joined in support, and a federal judge froze enforcement — making Colorado ground zero for the federal-vs-state AI regulation battle.

## 🔧 AI Tool & Product News
- **GPT-5.5 lands for paid users.** OpenAI began rolling out GPT-5.5 Thinking and GPT-5.5 Pro to Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise ChatGPT subscribers today, retiring GPT-4o and older models entirely. Context windows stretch to 400k tokens for Pro-tier Thinking users, and GPT-5.3 Instant now serves as the default fast model across all tiers. API access for GPT-5.5 is still pending, with OpenAI citing additional safety reviews before a wider release. [help.openai.com](https://help.openai.com/th-th/articles/11909943-gpt-53-and-gpt-55-in-chatgpt)
- **FLUX 2 brings sub-second images and 10-reference blending.** Black Forest Labs' FLUX 2 family is now fully available via Replicate's API, spanning FLUX.2 Max for premium product photography, a Pro tier with structured JSON prompting, and FLUX.2 Klein — a 4-billion-parameter model distilled for sub-second inference on consumer hardware. New editing tools like Fill, Depth, and Canny handle inpainting, retexturing, and sketch-to-image workflows. The suite supports up to 10 reference images for multi-image blending, a significant leap for character and product consistency. [replicate.com](https://replicate.com/collections/flux)
- **Runway Gen-4.5 tops video benchmarks as the field explodes.** Replicate's image-to-video collection now ranks Runway Gen-4.5 number one on the Artificial Analysis text-to-video benchmark, with Google's Veo 3.1 and ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 close behind. Veo 3.1 adds native audio — dialogue, sound effects, and ambient soundscapes — while Seedance 2.0 accepts up to nine reference images, three video clips, and three audio files in a single prompt. The pace of new model launches has made production-ready video generation a commodity API rather than a research demo. [replicate.com](https://replicate.com/collections/image-to-video)
- **Google opens Play Accelerator India for AI-powered startups.** Google has opened applications for the third cohort of Google Play Accelerator India, an equity-free three-month program offering mentorship, technical audits, and AI integration support to Indian app and game startups. Applications close June 1, with the program kicking off in July and a demo day set for November 2026. The initiative targets builders working on AI-native mobile products for a market that now has over 700 million smartphone users. [blog.google](https://blog.google/intl/en-in/company-news/empowering-indian-app-innovators-applications-open-for-google-play-accelerator-india/)

## 💰 AI Startup & Investment News
- **Suno eyes $5B+ valuation in new round.** AI music generation startup Suno is approaching the close of a funding round that would push its valuation above $5 billion, Axios reports. The company has become one of the most-used generative audio platforms since launching, competing with Udio and a growing field of music AI tools. The raise would cement Suno as one of the most highly valued AI-native consumer startups outside of the coding and enterprise categories. [axios.com](https://www.axios.com/pro/media-deals/2026/05/04/suno-ai-music-generation-5-billion)
- **Anduril closes in on $60B raise led by a16z and Thrive.** Defense-tech giant Anduril is finalizing a roughly $4 billion funding round that would value the company at $60 billion, with Andreessen Horowitz and Thrive Capital leading, according to The Information. A third investor is notable for the size of its check, though not yet named. The round reflects sustained investor appetite for AI-driven defense and autonomous systems as global defense budgets expand. [theinformation.com](https://www.theinformation.com/articles)
- **Starcloud targets $2.2B valuation for orbital data centers.** Two-year-old startup Starcloud, which is building data centers in orbit, is in talks to raise at least $200 million at a $2.2 billion post-money valuation — doubling its valuation from a $170 million round just one month ago. SpaceX's interest in the company has stirred additional investor attention as the space-compute category heats up. The raise is part of a broader wave of infrastructure bets tied to the assumption that terrestrial data center capacity will be a bottleneck for AI scaling. [theinformation.com](https://www.theinformation.com/articles)
- **Linkerbot doubles to $6B valuation on robotic hand demand.** Chinese robotics startup Linkerbot, the global market leader in dexterous robotic hands for humanoid robots, is seeking a $6 billion valuation in its next financing round — double the valuation it achieved in a round that just closed. The company's hands are increasingly being integrated into humanoid robot platforms from manufacturers competing to build general-purpose labor robots. Demand from automotive, logistics, and electronics assembly customers is driving the rapid re-rating. [jp.reuters.com](https://jp.reuters.com/video/watch/idRW754604052026RP1/)
- **Reserv lands $125M from KKR to automate insurance claims.** Reserv, an AI-driven insurance claims platform, announced a $125 million Series C led by KKR, targeting the slow-moving property and casualty claims market with automated workflows. The raise underscores continued investor conviction in vertical AI for regulated industries, where legacy processes remain largely manual. Reserv's technology handles claims analysis end-to-end, reducing cycle times and adjuster workload. [venturebeat.com](https://venturebeat.com/tag/industrial-automation)

## 🌐 General AI News
- **xAI sues Colorado, DOJ piles on, judge freezes enforcement.** xAI filed suit against Colorado's AI Act alleging First Amendment and equal protection violations, and the Department of Justice quickly moved to intervene — on xAI's side. A federal judge then approved a joint motion by xAI and the Colorado Attorney General to stay enforcement while the law is revised, marking the first time the federal government has actively backed a private company in blocking a state AI law. Colorado is now rewriting the Act to strip its algorithmic discrimination provisions, though legal observers note the revisions don't resolve all of xAI's constitutional claims. [thealgorithmicupdate.substack.com](https://thealgorithmicupdate.substack.com/p/may-2026-springtime-negotiations)
- **EU AI Act enforcement begins August 2026 — and most firms aren't ready.** Starting this August, organizations operating in Europe must classify their AI systems by risk level, conduct red-team adversarial testing, and publish transparency reports — or face fines of up to 7% of global turnover, higher than GDPR penalties. High-risk applications in employment, credit, education, and public services face the strictest scrutiny, including mandatory post-market monitoring. Legal experts warn that many mid-size firms are still behind on compliance, particularly on the red-team testing requirement, which demands documented and regulator-reviewable adversarial testing. [youtube.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A8kJ7I6BKKw)
- **Trump's AI order is freezing state legislatures — Congress steps in.** Virginia's General Assembly left multiple AI protection bills on the table after Trump's December executive order threatened to strip states of over $800 million in broadband funding if they passed 'onerous AI laws.' Bills covering companion chatbot harms to minors, AI in insurance claims, and mental health AI were all stalled or deferred to study committees. In response, Virginia's congressional delegation — including Sen. Mark Warner and Rep. Morgan Griffith — are advancing federal measures to fill the gaps, including the bipartisan GUARD Act protecting children from AI chatbots and the Workforce Transparency Act tracking AI's employment impact. [cardinalnews.org](https://cardinalnews.org/2026/05/04/ai-bills-faced-a-logjam-in-the-general-assembly-due-to-a-trump-executive-order-but-congress-is-working-to-fill-the-gap/)
- **China blocks Meta's $2B acquisition of agent startup Manus.** China's National Development and Reform Commission formally blocked Meta's $2 billion bid to acquire Manus, the Chinese AI agent startup, in what is described as the first state-level prohibition of an inbound AI acquisition by China. The decision signals Beijing's intent to keep high-value AI assets domestic as agent technology becomes a strategic priority. The move is likely to accelerate scrutiny of cross-border AI deals in both directions. [press.airstreet.com](https://press.airstreet.com/p/state-of-ai-may-2026)
